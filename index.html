About Us

Who We Are

SIMCO ENTERPRISE

SIMCO Enterprise has been a dynamic force in the Flexible Packaging Industry. We are proud to be the largest Polyethylene Packaging Industry in WEST AFRICA. We provides full service to virtually every industry in which customized flexible packaging is used: food, pharmaceutical, beverages, chemical, textile, airline, railroad, utilities,agricultural, retail and construction companies, - with prompt delivery and personalized service, we assure our customers' of great and complete satisfaction.

What We Do

Agricultural plastics films

The wide range of plasticulture applications includes greenhouse, mulch and silage stretch films, silage sheets, drip irrigation pipes, nettings, nonwovens and many more

Pure water films

we have the capability of meeting whatever pure water packaging needs the customer might have. We offer different grades of film, for different purposes.

Pure Water Cutting services

We offer a state of art rubber cutting services to pure water companies that will love to customize their sachet water packaging and also to any other company or individual that make use of plastic rubbers

Nylon wraps

we give our client numerous varieties of nylon to chose from. we also serve our customers with the best nylon material products

Customized printing

Speed matters in everything we do. We keep an eye on the clock, consistently accelerating our response times, reducing and eliminating dwell times, and delivering our products and services to you on your terms – on your schedule.
